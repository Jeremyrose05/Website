Welcome to Go Kartz

Go Kartz is an exceptional go-karting location where you and your friends can race at high speed. Go Kartz has top-of-the-line karts that go with one of the best tracks in the country.

Our new facility was built in 2020, is just outside the nation's capital, where you can bring teenagers from the age of 13 up to adult of the age of 55. Our goal, here at Go Kartz is to make your karting experience the best you ever had and become the best racer we ever had come to this location.

Here at Go Kartz we offer excellent hours with are highly trained staff. We have the best prices in the Gatineau/Ottawa region. We offer great customer support.We also have links to other go-karting place like us.
